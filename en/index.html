Who We Are

Built on Trade,
Grounded in Trust

Active Global Impex was established in 2008 in Gandhidham — a major commodity trading centre in Gujarat's Kutch district, adjacent to Mundra, one of India's largest commercial ports. Since then, we have grown from a regional trader into an export partner for buyers across Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

Our aim is simple: source right, pack right, ship on time. Shipments are typically accompanied by quality certificates, inspection reports, and export documentation — subject to product type, buyer requirements, and the terms agreed in each sales contract.
